About Me

My name's Thomas, and I'm excited to share some of the things I'm interested in. I'm an avid webdesign enthusiast, so my favorite languages are HTML, CSS, and Javascript. When I'm not deciding on which colors or the layout I should use, you can catch me listening to some of my favorite genres of music, namely instrumentals when I'm trying to focus but if I'm on a run or cycling EDM will do the trick. Outside of my web design, cycling, and music life, I sometimes like to kick back and play some Super Smash Bros. Melee (even though I'm not really good at it) and am a Falco enjoyer.
